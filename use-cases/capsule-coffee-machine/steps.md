# Capsule Coffee Machine — Disassembly Steps

Video reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5E5DRJqs9JU

---

## Phase 1 — Remove external parts

Start by removing the loose external parts — no tools needed for any of this.

1. Lift the **water tank** straight up and out.
2. Pull the **capsule support** forward and detach it.
3. Remove any other **external supports** by hand.

---

## Phase 2 — Remove the outer casing

> **Tool required: T20 Torx screwdriver**

1. Unscrew the **2 T20 Torx screws** located near the top lever.
2. Before continuing, make sure the **lever is in the closed position**. If it isn't, close it and try again.
3. Pull off the **front cover piece**.
4. Pry off **side panel A** by releasing the clips at the rear.
5. Repeat for **side panel B**.
6. Swivel and lift off the **top cover**.

The inner skeleton is now exposed.

---

## Phase 3 — Disconnect the wiring

1. Push the tab in and remove the **power cable connector**.
2. Unclip the **wires** from their routing tabs along the frame.
3. Unplug the **ground cable**.
4. Pull out the **complete power cord**.

The inner housing is now accessible.

---

## Phase 4 — Open the inner housing

### Remove the water tank nozzle mask

1. Release the **first two side tabs**.
2. Release the remaining tabs and slide the **nozzle mask** off.

### Remove the side housing

3. Work your way along the frame and unfasten all the **inner housing side tabs** from one end to the other.
4. Wiggle the **side housing** free.

The brewing mechanism is now accessible.

---

## Phase 5 — Extract the brewing mechanism

At this point, decide whether you need to do a **deep clean**:

- **No deep clean needed** — you're done. The capsule perforator area is now exposed for light cleaning.
- **Deep clean needed** — continue to Phase 6.

---

## Phase 6 — Disassemble the brewing unit *(deep clean only)*

1. Open the **top lever** to get access to the front inner area.
2. Close the lever back.
3. Push the **brewing mechanism inward** to align it with the release slot.
4. Pull the **complete brewing mechanism** out.

Once the mechanism is out:

5. Undo the **two tabs on each side** of the unit.
6. Push the back part inward and remove the **swivel joints**.
7. Release the remaining tabs to **free the inner assembly**.
8. Pop open the **final chamber** by releasing the top and bottom tabs.

The **capsule perforator area** is now fully exposed and ready for cleaning.
